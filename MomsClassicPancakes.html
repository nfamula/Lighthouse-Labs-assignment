Mom's Classic Pancakes




  • what you will need
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 cups buttermilk
  • <1/4 cup oil
  • 1 3/4 cups flour
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 2 teaspoons magic baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon salt



what to do

Heat non-stick pan to medium high heat. In a large bowl beat eggs. Stir in buttermilk and oil. Add remaining ingredients just until large lumps disappear.



Lightly grease pan. A few drops of water on pan sizzle and bounce when heat is just right. Pour batter 1/4 cup at a time onto pan. Cook until bubbles form and egdes start to dry.



Makes sixteen 4-inch delicious pancakes!



tips and tricks

Do not use sweet milk! If you absolutely cannot find buttermilk in your neighbourhood, decrease milk to 1 3/4 cups, increase baking powder to 4 teaspoons, and omit baking soda.

Yummy extras: 1/2 cup shredded apples and 1/2 teaspoon cinnamon added to batter, or 1 cup frozen --thawed and drained-- or fresh blueberries.

Bon Appetit!
